Overview of Astrological Signs

The zodiac system consists of 12 astrological signs, each representing unique traits and characteristics. From Aries (Mesh) to Pisces (Meen), these signs play a crucial role in shaping your personality and destiny. Understanding your Rashi can provide clarity on your strengths, weaknesses, and potential life events. Here's a quick overview of the 12 signs and their key attributes:

  • Aries (Mesh): Bold, energetic, and ambitious.
  • Taurus (Vrishabh): Reliable, patient, and determined.
  • Gemini (Mithun): Curious, adaptable, and communicative.
  • Cancer (Kark): Emotional, nurturing, and intuitive.
  • Leo (Sinh): Confident, charismatic, and creative.
  • Virgo (Kanya): Analytical, practical, and detail-oriented.
  • Libra (Tula): Diplomatic, charming, and balanced.
  • Scorpio (Vrishchik): Intense, passionate, and resourceful.
  • Sagittarius (Dhanu): Optimistic, adventurous, and philosophical.
  • Capricorn (Makar): Disciplined, ambitious, and responsible.
  • Aquarius (Kumbh): Innovative, independent, and humanitarian.
  • Pisces (Meen): Compassionate, imaginative, and spiritual.

Each of these signs will experience unique influences in 2025. Let's dive into the monthly rashi bhavishya predictions to see what the year holds for you.
